How to start School-y

W
Windows: Double-click School-y.vbs — runs silently, no terminal window.
M
Mac: Double-click start.command — Terminal flashes briefly, then browser opens.
C
Chromebook: Open the Linux terminal, run bash setup-chromebook.sh once — then School-y appears in your app launcher forever. Just click it like any app.
4
Once running, this page redirects automatically — or click Open School-y above.

First launch installs packages and may take ~30 seconds. After that it's instant.

Requires Node.js to be installed.
